About this site

Division 3.1 of Bill21-Forest and Range Practices Amendment Act requires Forest Stewardship Plan holders to make a Forest Operations Map available for public review and comment. Public engagement in the management of Crown resources is encouraged and this site offers a platform for members of the public to submit comments for consideration.

This site contains Forest Operations Maps posted by the Province as of [TODO: XXX date]. Once an Forest Operations Maps has been reviewed and a decision made, it will remain on the site for a limited period of time.

For Forest Operations Maps posted prior to [TODO: XXX date], please refer to the Applications and Reasons for Decision (ARFD) website (listed below).

Forest Operations Map Status

Forest Operations Map progress through three phases: publication, public review and comment, final submission

  1. Publication: The Forest Operations Map is published on the site.
  2. Public Review and Comment: Comments on the Forest Operations Map can be submitted through the site.
  3. Final Submission: Comments received by the Forest Stewardship Plan holder are considered and where applicable incorporated into the final submission of the Forest Operations Map and associated report.

Decision

  1. Abandoned: The Forest Operations Map review and processing has been cancelled or abandoned (by either the client or applicant). No further action will be taken.
